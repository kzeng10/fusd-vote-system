Welcome to the FUSD Speaking Indicator System


Click here to indicate whether you would like to speak! Click here to go to the admin page!


How it works:

If you wish to speak, click on the link for the speak page. Enter in your name and whether you want to speak, and hit submit.

If you wish to moderate the system, click on the link for the admin page. The page will populate with everyone who sent a request so far. You may refresh the page to update who wants to speak or not if someone submits after you load the page. If you want to reset the system, hit reset, and wait for everyone to submit before you refresh the page.

Special thanks to the Fremont Unified School District for giving us the opportunity to work on this web application!

Extra note: this web application can also be used as a live polling/querying system, as long as you just change "speak" to "vote". :)
